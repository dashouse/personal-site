Product Design Lead (Design System)

March 2020 – November 2021

Leading the creation of a brand new multi-brand design system ‘Loop’ for the largest telecoms group in the UK.

Big wins (for the team):

  • Created a headless design system that retained the individuality and personality of each BT brand (BT, EE, Plusnet)
  • Migrated hundreds of designers and engineers to a brand new system and ways of working
  • Used the Design System to connect previously siloed areas of the organisation
  • Developed a community “component blueprints” library as a way to share and iterate on component and pattern design before design system contribution
  • As an Accessibility Guild member, I increased knowledge of accessible interface design within the design community
